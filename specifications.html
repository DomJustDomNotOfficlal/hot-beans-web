Lead Developer

We're looking for a very experienced Web Developer to lead our offices in the UK. Hot Beans Web is a fastly growing Web Development company that is part of the Hot Beans Group. We are expanding from 1 office to 2 in the next year, and we need a Lead Developer to lead the growth. If not we would appreciate an experienced Developer at best.

Job Role:

  • Architect and oversee the design of scalable, maintainable web applications and systems
  • Set and enforce coding standards, best practices, and development workflows across the team
  • Make key technology decisions, including frameworks, languages, tools, and third-party integrations
  • Conduct and approve code reviews to ensure quality and consistency
  • Lead, mentor, and support a team of junior and mid-level developers
  • Delegate tasks effectively, balancing workload and individual strengths
  • Run daily standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives
  • Identify skills gaps and support team learning and professional development
  • Collaborate with project managers to define timelines, scope, and technical feasibility
  • Break down complex requirements into actionable development tasks
  • Monitor progress and remove blockers to keep projects on track
  • Ensure deliverables meet client requirements, performance benchmarks, and deadlines

Salary:

  • £50,000

Our Benefits:

  • Free Hot Beans Coffee
  • Ability to work from any of the offices
  • Discounts for all Major Companies
  • Full-Time Working Hours
  • Stable Salary - No Payrises and Pension Schemes

Qualifications and Experience:

  • A degree related to computer science or an equivalent qualification
  • Experience in leading a company expansion
  • 1+ Years as a Lead Developer and 5+ Years Experience as a Senior Developer
  • Being able to work independently and as a team
  • Being proactive and fixing problems
